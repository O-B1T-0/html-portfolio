The Best Movies According to Seyi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception (directed by Christopher Nolan)

Genre: sci-fi, heist action
Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a highly-skilled thief, sought after all over the world, by both interested clients and law enforcement authorities, does whatever he must to constantly survive, to someday find a way back to his family. So, when offered the chance to be reunited with them, he must accept a mission unlike any other before it. It's not jewels he's hired to steal, though...

Avengers: Infinity War (directed by The Russo Brothers)

Genre: superhero action
When Thanos (Josh Brolin) embarks on his spiritual quest to balance the universe using the Infinity Gauntlet, the Avengers, led by Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr.), Steve Rogers (Chris Evans), and Thor Odinson (Chris Hemsworth), are forced into a race against the clock to ensure that does not happen. Will they succeed?

The Devil Wears Prada (directed by David Frankel)

Genre: comedy, romance
Andrea Bowen (Anne Hathaway), a plucky, fresh undergraduate of Northwestern University, is determined to achieve her dreams of becoming a successful journalist. Where best to intern, in order to achieve this? Runway Magazine, of course. Except, there, she must navigate office life & politics on a daily basis, while working under her perfectionist boss Miranda Priestly, its legendary editor-in-chief. Is Andrea's career over before it's even begun?
